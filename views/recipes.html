    JIM BEAM ORANGE JULEP

    INGREDIENTES

    2 parts Jim Beam® Orange

    5-6 mint leaves

    2 dashes aromatic bitters

    0.5 part simple syrup

    Garnish with orange wedge and mint sprig

    PREPARACION

    In a mint julep cup or rocks glass, gently muddle the mint, bitters and syrup. Add crushed ice and Jim Beam® Orange. Stir to combine topping with more ice as needed.

    JIM BEAM ORANGE CRUSH

    INGREDIENTES

    1.5 parts Jim Beam® Orange

    0.5 part orange juice

    0.75 part lemon juice

    0.5 part simple syrup

    Soda water

    Garnish with orange wedge and mint sprig

    PREPARACION

    Add all ingredients (except soda water) to a cocktail shaker with ice. Shake and strain into a collins glass over ice. Top with soda water and gently stir.

    JIM BEAM ORANGE YOU GLAD

    INGREDIENTES

    1.5 parts Jim Beam® Orange

    0.5 part simple syrup

    1 part pineapple juice

    0.5 part lime juice

    2 dashes aromatic bitters

    Garnish with orange and pineapple wedge and maraschino cherry

    PREPARACION

    Add all ingredients to a cocktail shaker with ice. Shake until well chilled and strain into a rocks glass over fresh ice.

    JIM BEAM ORANGE GINGER SOUR

    INGREDIENTES

    1.5 parts Jim Beam® Orange

    0.75 part ginger syrup

    0.75 part lemon juice

    Garnish with orange or lemon wedge and candied ginger

    PREPARACION

    First, make your ginger syrup. Bring ginger, sugar and water to a simmer in a small saucepan. Stir until sugar has dissolved, then simmer for 30 minutes. Strain out the ginger and let the syrup come to room temperature.

    For the cocktail, combine all ingredients in a cocktail shaker with ice. Shake until well chilled and strain into a cocktail glass over ice.

    JIM BEAM ORANGE MULE

    INGREDIENTES

    1.5 parts Jim Beam® Orange

    Lemon wedge

    0.5 part simple syrup

    4 parts ginger beer

    Garnish with orange wedge and mint sprig

    PREPARACION

    Squeeze and drop a lemon wedge into a cocktail shaker with ice. Add all ingredients (except the ginger beer) and shake. Strain into a collins glass or mule mug over ice. Top with ginger beer and gently stir.

    JIM BEAM BOURBON CREAM HOT CHOCOLATE

    INGREDIENTES

    2 parts Jim Beam® Bourbon Cream

    6 parts hot water

    1 packet of hot cocoa mix

    PREPARACION

    Stir together the cocoa mix and hot water in a mug. Add Bourbon Cream and give it another quick stir. Top with cinnamon, whipped cream or marshmallows.

    JIM BEAM® CITRUS HIGHBALL

    INGREDIENTES

    45ml Jim Beam® White Label

    20ml fresh lemon juice

    Soda water

    Crushed ice

    Slice of lemon

    Sprig of rosemary

    PREPARACION

    Pour 45ml of Jim Beam® White Label over a packed glass of ice. Add 20ml of fresh lemon juice. Top with cold soda water. Stir with a long spoon and enjoy. Garnish with a lemon slice and a sprig of rosemary.

    JIM BEAM® BOURBON SOUR

    INGREDIENTES

    2 parts Jim Beam® Bourbon

    1 part Lemon Juice

    0.5 parts Lemon Lime Soda

    0.50 Teaspoon Sugar

    Lemon wedge and cherry

    PREPARACION

    Mix Jim Beam® with lemon juice, sugar, and ice in shaker. Shake vigorously and strain into whiskey sour glass. Garnish with lemon wedge and cherry.
